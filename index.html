About me

I have been learning to code since I was 12 and have entered and won multiple coding competitions since. I majored in computer science and have two specializations, one in Machine Learning and another one in Videogames using Unity.

Additionally, my background in machine learning and computer vision, developed during my master's and various research projects, such as the Bird Song analysis at UCLA and video surveillance systems at ITESM CEM, underscores my capability to innovate and apply state of the art technologies to real-world applications. My project that utilized an open API to map the shortest routes between two points showcases my ability to integrate novel technologies, a skill I am eager to bring to the Developer Experience.
